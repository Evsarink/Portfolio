About

Built with curiosity. Shaped with care.

I’m someone who loves building things that feel calm, useful, and alive. My work lives at the intersection of design, code, and quiet experimentation. I’m happiest when I’m sketching an interface, writing a thoughtful line of CSS, or turning an idea into an experience people genuinely enjoy.

What keeps me going is the feeling of making something real — something that can be held, read, used, and remembered. Whether I’m crafting a website, refining an interaction, or learning a new approach, I try to leave a little warmth behind.
